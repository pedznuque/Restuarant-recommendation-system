A Metro Manila Resto Finder Using Content-Based Recommendation System Utilizing Cosine Similarity and TF-IDF Algorithm

-About us-
As students pursuing careers in the field of computer science and technology, we understand the importance of developing efficient and effective systems that can improve various aspects of our daily lives. One area that has been consistently growing and evolving is the restaurant recommendation system.

The restaurant recommendation system that we have developed is a unique and innovative solution that combines the latest technologies in machine learning and data analysis to provide users with the most relevant recommendations.
With this system, users are able to enter their desired location and cuisine preferences, as well as their preferred price range. This system then utilizes a TF-IDF vectorization method and cosine similarity calculations to provide the user with a list of the most relevant restaurants that match their specifications.

The advantages of this system are numerous. Firstly, the system provides a much more personalized experience for users as it takes into account their specific location, cuisine and price range preferences. This leads to a higher degree of satisfaction as the recommendations provided are more closely aligned with the user's individual tastes and needs.

Additionally, the use of machine learning and data analysis helps to mitigate the problem of information overload, as users are no longer required to sift through countless pages of information to find the right restaurant. Instead, they are presented with a concise and well-organized list of the top recommendations that best fit their specifications.
