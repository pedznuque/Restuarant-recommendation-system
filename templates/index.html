-About Us-

As students pursuing careers in the field of computer science and technology, we understand the importance of developing efficient and effective systems that can improve various aspects of our daily lives.
One area that has been consistently growing and evolving is the restaurant recommendation system. The restaurant recommendation system that we have developed is a unique and innovative solution that combines the latest technologies in machine learning and data analysis to provide users with the most relevant recommendations. With this system, users are able to enter their desired location and cuisine preferences, as well as their preferred price range. This system then utilizes a TF-IDF vectorization method and cosine similarity calculations to provide the user with a list of the most relevant restaurants that match their specifications.
